Our Leadership

Shri Ramavtar Jogi

President

A retired Indian Army officer and dedicated social servant, Shri Ramavtar Jogi has worked with VHP, Hindu Yuva Vahini, Gau Rakshak Dal, and many others. His life is committed to service and unity in the community engagement and leadership.

He has a deep understanding of how to effectively manage and unite members of the community. Shri Jogi has also been associated with several esteemed social and religious organizations, including Vishwa Hindu Parishad, Hindu Yuva Vahini, Gau Rakshak Dal among others. His continued commitment to public service reflects his unwavering dedication to the welfare and upliftment of society .

Shri Anoop Jogi

General Secretary

Shri Anoop Jogi is a dedicated and compassionate social worker, committed to bringing about positive change in society. As the General Secretary of Akhil Bharatvarshiya Jogi/Nath Maha Sabha, he oversees the organization's operations and strategic planning. With a strong background in social services and Akhil Bharatvarshiya Jogi/Nath Maha Sabha, he plays a vital role in leading initiatives aimed at community upliftment and social development.

Under his guidance, our team continues to work tirelessly to empower individuals, strengthen social unity, and promote cultural values. His commitment and leadership reflect the core mission of the Sabha — to serve, support, and unify the Jogi/Nath community across the nation.

Shri Parmanand

Treasurer

Shri Parmanand, a Postgraduate in Computer Science, brings both academic excellence and a strong sense of responsibility to his role as Treasurer of Akhil Bharatvarshiya Jogi/Nath Maha Sabha. With his analytical skills and disciplined approach, he ensures the efficient and transparent management of the Sabha’s financial affairs.

His commitment to accountability, integrity, and service makes him a valuable asset to the organization. Shri Parmanand continues to contribute towards the Sabha’s mission of unity, progress, and social upliftment of the Jogi/Nath community.

About Sabha / महासभा के बारे में

Akhil Bharatvarshiya Jogi/Nath MahaSabha is a national-level initiative for unity, culture, and upliftment of the Jogi/Joginath community.

अखिल भारतवर्षीय जोगी/नाथ महासभा का उद्देश्य समाज की एकता, सांस्कृतिक पहचान और सामाजिक विकास को बढ़ावा देना है।
